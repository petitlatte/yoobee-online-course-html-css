Celebrate Hutt Japan Day

Sunday, November 18, 2018
11:00 AM 3:00 PM
Dowse Art Museum
45 Laings Road Lower Hutt, Wellington, 5010

Japan Day is a mini-festival full of Japanese cultural and sporting performances, language, dance, food-trucks and your chance to share in the culture of our sister city Minoh in Osaka, Japan.

Get a taste of Japan with taiko drumming and martial arts performances plus food trucks and stalls including Gogo Takoyaki, Mao & Co, Matsuda Shouten Japanese food truck, One-Bites Gourmet and Taiyaki. Inside discover Japanese arts and crafts, cultural groups, watch dancing and singing performances, talk to our friends in Minoh via our permanent live Skype to Minoh link and pose for the Amazing Traveling Photo Booth.
