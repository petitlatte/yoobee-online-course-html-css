Tinkerbelle

Tinkerbelle's owner was no longer able to care for her, so she was taken in a a friend. Unfortunately, the incumbent cat was not happy with Tinkerbelle's arrival and began to bully her. She's come to the shelter for a chance at a new life. In her previous home, Tinkerbelle spent most of her time indoors. She is shy initially, but with a little patience and a slow approach, she really enjoys being patted.

Tinkerbelle has lived with another cat happily, and seems comfortable with the other cat friendly cats at the shelter, so may be fine living with another quiet, cat-friendly cat.

Tinkerbelle would suit a quiet, stable home where she doesn't have to go through more upheaval. Tinkerbelle's adoption fee is $80
